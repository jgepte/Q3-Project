Fire Safety

Never leave your kitchen unattended whilst cooking

-Leaving the kitchen on, even if only for a few seconds puts your entire house at risk, it's best to call someone or be near to the area if you have unattended bussiness.
Always be accompanied with a blanket or fire extinguisher

-In case an actual fire does happen around your house for some reason, you should already be ready for one in case to put it out or halt it's spread temporarily.
Maintain or keep your wires safe

-Before you plug in or plug out a wire, it's very important to check the current state of your wires and make sure they're not chewed or torn or stepped on. If you find any of your wires are in a bad state make sure to find new replacements fast as broken wires can spark a fire especially once they're plugged in.
Store any flammable products

-Flammable products in your house like household cleaners such as hand sanitizers, mosquito sprays, and many more can react strongly to any source of heat if nearby which is why they should be stored in cabinets or in other areas of your house and should be nowhere nearby areas like the kitchen.
